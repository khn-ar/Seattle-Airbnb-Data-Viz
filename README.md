# About Project
- Airbnb dataset for the city of Seattle
- Data vizualization on Tableau
- Analytics dashboard

# Analysis
- Average bedroom prices - *bar chart*
- Distinct count of bedroom listings - *table*
- Price per Zipcode - *map*
- Price by Zipcode - *bar chart*
- Revenue for year - *line chart*

# Insights
- Average pricing consistently increases as bedroom count increases
- Highest average pricings near coastal, city centre areas
- Highest revenue at the end of year vs. lowest at the beginning of the year
